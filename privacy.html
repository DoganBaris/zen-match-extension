Privacy Policy for Zen Match

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Data Collection

Zen Match does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data (scores, level progress) remains entirely on your local device and is never sent anywhere.

The extension has no backend server, and no data ever leaves your machine.

2. Permissions Used

The extension requires one permission to provide its core functionality:

  • storage — used exclusively to save your game progress locally (best level, best score, total games played, and current level). This data is stored in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted externally.

Zen Match does not access your browsing history, tabs, or any website data. It is a self-contained puzzle game that runs entirely within its own window.

3. Local-Only Operation

All game data stored by this extension:

  • Is saved only on your local device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is never shared with any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by removing the extension

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. It makes no network requests of any kind.
